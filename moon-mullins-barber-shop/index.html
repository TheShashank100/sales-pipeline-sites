A Corinth Institution

Moon Mullins' Barber Shop has been a fixture on Franklin Street since 1946. Through decades of change in Corinth, one thing has remained constant — men and boys have come here for a clean cut, an honest price, and good conversation.

This is a no-frills, no-appointment, walk-right-in barbershop. The kind your grandfather went to. The kind that's almost impossible to find anymore. We've kept the craft alive because that's what Corinth deserves.

No booking app, no wait list, no nonsense. Just sit down and we'll take care of you like we always have.
